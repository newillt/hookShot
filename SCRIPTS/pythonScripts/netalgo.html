    Welcome to NETALGO

    NETALGO is a tool that will allow you to quickly and easily create models from your data. This is a work in progress, so check back often and you will see new features being added. If you have some suggestions, please drop me a line and let me know.

    NETALGO will make Response Surface models available to everyone from finance to engineering through a simple web interface. Response Surface Models (RSM) are powerful mathematical models that can capture complex interactions. RSMs have been used in many industries for a long time, RSM functionality is available in many software packages, but the software is complex and the interaction with data is inefficient.
